Our Students

The B.Tech. in CSE is a broad and flexible degree program with the curriculum specifically designed to reflect the depth and breadth of computer science. Expert members from Academia and Industry provide inputs in introducing specialized courses in the curriculum to suit industry needs. To further enhance the quality of the programs, the department has academic collaborations with international professors from universities like Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburg, USA.
